## Use Cases

- Website SEO health check
- Content optimization recommendations
- Technical SEO issue diagnosis
- Competitor SEO analysis
- Keyword strategy development

## Core Capabilities

- **Technical Audit**: Site structure, speed, mobile
- **Content Audit**: Keywords, meta tags, content quality
- **Link Analysis**: Internal and external link optimization
- **Competitor Comparison**: Analyze competitor strategies

- Avoid black hat techniques
