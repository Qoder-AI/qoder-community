## Use Cases

- New product launches
- Major version releases
- Product Hunt launches
- Feature announcements
- Brand refreshes

## Core Capabilities

- **Timeline Planning**: Pre and post-launch cadence
- **Channel Strategy**: Multi-channel coordination
- **Content Preparation**: Launch materials checklist
- **Community Mobilization**: Early user engagement

## Example

```
Please plan the Qoder 2.0 launch strategy:

Product: Major AI coding assistant update
Launch date: 2 weeks from now
Goals:
- Product Hunt homepage
- 1000 new signups
- Media coverage

Provide complete launch plan.
```

## Notes

- Prepare all materials in advance
- Coordinate team collaboration
- Prepare for various scenarios
- Continue follow-up after launch
