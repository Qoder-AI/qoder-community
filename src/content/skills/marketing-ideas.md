## Use Cases

- Marketing brainstorming
- Growth strategy exploration
- Creative campaign planning
- Differentiation positioning
- Breaking growth plateaus

## Core Capabilities

- **Idea Generation**: Multi-angle marketing ideas
- **Feasibility Assessment**: Resources and implementation difficulty
- **Case References**: Successful case inspiration
- **Priority Suggestions**: Potential impact ranking

## Example

```
I need to brainstorm marketing ideas for Qoder:

Current state:
- Monthly active users: 5000
- Customer acquisition cost: $50
- Main channel: Organic search

Goal: Double users in 3 months

Provide 10 creative marketing ideas, ranked by potential impact.
```

## Notes

- Ideas should be actionable
- Consider resource constraints
- Maintain brand consistency
- Can start with small-scale testing
