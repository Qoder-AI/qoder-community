## Use Cases

- Evaluate ecommerce landing pages, product detail pages, campaign pages, cart/checkout flows for design quality
- Review banners, popups, modals, and recommendation cards against industry best practices
- Conduct systematic UX audits with 70+ checklist items across 7 dimensions
- Get actionable design optimization suggestions backed by Baymard Institute and Nielsen Norman Group research
- Benchmark designs against major platform patterns (Taobao, JD, Pinduoduo, Douyin)

## Example

```text
帮我评估一下这个电商落地页的设计质量，看看有哪些可以优化的地方。
```

```text
Review this product detail page design against ecommerce best practices.
```

## Dependencies

- Commonly used together with **design-predictor** (which calls this skill internally for design quality assessment in step 6)
- Also pairs well with **ecommerce-ctr-evaluator** for comprehensive design + CTR analysis

## Notes

- Covers 7 evaluation dimensions: information architecture, visual hierarchy, cognitive psychology, conversion paths, brand consistency, AI-assisted design, and accessibility/compliance
- Includes 19+ real-world case studies from major ecommerce platforms
- Contains 8 design pattern libraries for common ecommerce UI components
- Supports evaluation of: landing pages, product detail pages (PDP), campaign pages, cart/checkout pages, banners, popups/modals, and recommendation cards
- Checklist items are based on Baymard Institute, Nielsen Norman Group, and major platform (Taobao, JD, Pinduoduo, Douyin) design guidelines
