## Use Cases

- Evaluate CTR performance against industry benchmarks for specific channels and positions
- Compare click-through rates across industry verticals and device types
- Generate structured assessment reports with ratings and optimization recommendations
- Benchmark ad campaign CTR against platform-specific standards

## Example

```text
评估一下我们首页 Banner 的 CTR 表现，当前是 2.3%，行业水平怎么样？
```

## Notes

- Covers multiple advertising channels: search ads, display ads, social media, email, and on-site placements
- Includes benchmarks segmented by page position, industry vertical, and device/platform
- Provides clear rating system with actionable optimization suggestions
- Reference data and examples included in the skill repository
