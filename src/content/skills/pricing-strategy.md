## Use Cases

- SaaS pricing design
- Price tier planning
- Competitor price analysis
- Promotional strategies
- Price sensitivity testing

## Core Capabilities

- **Value-based Pricing**: Price based on customer value
- **Tier Design**: Reasonable feature tiering
- **Competitive Analysis**: Market positioning
- **Psychological Pricing**: Price presentation techniques

## Example

```
Please design pricing strategy for Qoder:

Product: AI coding assistant
Target users: Independent developers, startups, enterprises
Competitor pricing:
- Cursor: $20/month
- GitHub Copilot: $19/month

Design:
- 3-4 price tiers
- Feature allocation per tier
- Pricing recommendations and rationale
- Annual discount strategy
```

## Notes

- Pricing affects brand positioning
- Allow for future price adjustments
- Consider international market differences
- Keep it simple and transparent
